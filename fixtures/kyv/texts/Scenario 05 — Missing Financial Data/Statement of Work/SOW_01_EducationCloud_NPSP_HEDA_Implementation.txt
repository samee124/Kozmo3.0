Provider: Salesforce, Inc. Effective Date: March 17, 2025 1. Purpose This Statement of Work ("SOW") is entered into pursuant to the Agreement and describes the professional services to be provided by SFDC's designated implementation partner to configure Education Cloud, Non-Profit Success Pack (NPSP), and Higher Education Data Architecture (HEDA) for Customer's Office of Information Technology, Enrollment Management, and University Advancement teams. 2. Scope of Services • Discovery and current-state assessment of existing Salesforce org and data model • Configuration of HEDA data architecture (Accounts, Contacts, Affiliations, Educational relationships) • Configuration of NPSP-based Advancement/Gift Entry module for the University Advancement office • Migration of legacy constituent records from existing systems into the HEDA data model • User training for OIIT administrators and designated departmental power users • Post-go-live hypercare support (30 days) 3. Deliverables Discovery Report, Solution Design Document, Configured Production Org, Data Migration Summary, Training Materials, and Go-Live Readiness Checklist. 4. Timeline Estimated duration: 16 weeks from kickoff. Kickoff is targeted for April 2025, with go-live targeted for Q3 2025, subject to Customer resource availability and change requests. 5.
