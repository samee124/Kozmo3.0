AMENDMENT NO. 2 TO THE MASTER SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT Term Extension and Scope Amendment This Amendment No. 2 ("Amendment") is entered into as of the Effective Date set forth below, by and between Salesforce, Inc. ("SFDC"), as successor in interest to certain obligations of Salesforce.org under the Master Subscription Agreement dated June 29, 2018, as amended by the U.S. Public Sector and Education Amendment dated December 19, 2024 (collectively, the "Agreement"), and Nova Southeastern University, Inc. ("Customer"). 1. Background Customer and SFDC desire to extend the term of the Agreement to support Customer's continued use of the Services across its Office of Information Technology, Enrollment Management, and Advancement business units, and to expand the scope of Services to include Education Cloud and associated Non-Profit Success Pack (NPSP) and Higher Education Data Architecture (HEDA) functionality, as further detailed in the applicable Order Form(s) and the accompanying Statement of Work referenced in Section 3 below. 2. Term Section 11.1 of the Agreement is hereby amended to extend the Term through June 30, 2028, subject to the renewal provisions of Section 11.2 of the Agreement, as modified by the PS-EDU Amendment. Pricing for the extended Term, including any applicable Order Forms and associated fees, shall be governed exclusively by the Order Form(s) executed by the parties from time to time and is not restated herein. 3. Scope of Services The parties acknowledge that a Statement of Work for the implementation of Education Cloud, NPSP, and HEDA functionality (the "SOW") will govern the professional services engagement supporting this expanded scope. Fees, milestones, and payment terms applicable to the SOW are set forth exclusively in the SOW and any associated Order Form, and are incorporated herein by reference. 4. Contacts Customer's primary administrative contact for purposes of this Amendment is the Office of Information Technology (OIIT), care of the AVP of Enterprise Applications. Customer's prior billing contact of record (T. Shields) is noted as no longer current; Customer agrees to provide an updated billing contact under separate notice in accordance with Section 12.6 (Notices) of the Agreement. 5. No Other Changes Except as expressly modified herein, all other terms and conditions of the Agreement remain in full force and effect. SALESFORCE, INC. NOVA SOUTHEASTERN UNIVERSITY, INC.
