This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") governs the processing of personal data by Arclight Cloud Solutions, Inc. ("Processor") on behalf of a customer ("Controller") in connection with the Arclight platform. 2. PROCESSOR OBLIGATIONS. Processor shall: (a) process personal data only on documented instructions from Controller; (b) ensure persons authorized to process personal data are subject to confidentiality obligations; (c) implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures; (d) assist Controller in responding to data subject requests; and (e) notify Controller without undue delay upon becoming aware of a personal data breach. 3. SUB-PROCESSORS. Processor maintains a current list of sub-processors, available upon request, and will provide notice of any intended changes. 4. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS. Where personal data is transferred outside the European Economic Area, the parties agree to rely on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses or another valid transfer mechanism. 5. DELETION/RETURN. Upon termination of the underlying agreement, Processor will delete or return all personal data, at Controller's election, within a commercially reasonable period.
