Vendor Security Questionnaire Responses Provided By: Arclight Cloud Solutions, Inc. Prepared for prospective customer security due diligence review Completed: June 10, 2025 Respondent: Priya Anand, Director of Security & Compliance, Arclight Cloud Solutions, Inc.
1. Organizational Security Question 1 of 50 Q1. Does your organization have a formal, documented information security policy? A: Yes. Arclight maintains a formal Information Security Policy, reviewed and approved annually by the CISO and executive leadership, and distributed to all personnel. Page 2 of 51
1. Organizational Security Question 2 of 50 Q2. Is there a designated individual or team responsible for information security? A: Yes. Arclight employs a dedicated Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) and a Security team of 6 FTEs responsible for the security program. Page 3 of 51
1. Organizational Security Question 3 of 50 Q3. Do you conduct periodic security awareness training for employees? A: Yes. All employees complete mandatory security awareness training upon hire and annually thereafter, including phishing simulation exercises. Page 4 of 51
1. Organizational Security Question 4 of 50 Q4. Do you perform background checks on employees with access to customer data? A: Yes. Background checks are performed on all employees prior to hire, to the extent permitted by applicable law, and are re-verified for personnel in sensitive roles every three years. Page 5 of 51
1. Organizational Security Question 5 of 50 Q5. Is there a formal onboarding and offboarding process for personnel with system access? A: Yes. Access provisioning and deprovisioning follow a documented process tied to HR events, with access revoked within 24 hours of termination. Page 6 of 51
2. Access Control Question 6 of 50 Q6. Is multi-factor authentication (MFA) required for access to production systems? A: Yes. MFA is enforced for all administrative and production system access without exception. Page 7 of 51
2. Access Control Question 7 of 50 Q7. Do you follow the principle of least privilege for access provisioning? A: Yes. Access is granted based on documented role-based access control (RBAC) policies and reviewed quarterly. Page 8 of 51
2. Access Control Question 8 of 50 Q8. Are access rights reviewed periodically? A: Yes. User access rights are reviewed on a quarterly basis by system and data owners, with remediation tracked to closure. Page 9 of 51
2. Access Control Question 9 of 50 Q9. Is privileged access separately monitored and logged? A: Yes. All privileged account activity is logged and monitored via a centralized SIEM with alerting on anomalous behavior. Page 10 of 51
2. Access Control Question 10 of 50 Q10. Do you support single sign-on (SSO) integration for customer administrators? A: Yes. Arclight supports SAML 2.0-based SSO integration with major identity providers. Page 11 of 51
3. Data Security Question 11 of 50 Q11. Is customer data encrypted at rest? A: Yes. All customer data at rest is encrypted using AES-256. Page 12 of 51
3. Data Security Question 12 of 50 Q12. Is customer data encrypted in transit? A: Yes. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. Page 13 of 51
3. Data Security Question 13 of 50 Q13. Do you support customer-managed encryption keys? A: Yes, for Enterprise-tier customers. Standard-tier customers use Arclight-managed keys via AWS KMS. Page 14 of 51
3. Data Security Question 14 of 50 Q14. Is customer data logically or physically segregated from other customers? A: Customer data is logically segregated using tenant-scoped access controls and per- tenant encryption keys within a multi-tenant architecture. Page 15 of 51
3. Data Security Question 15 of 50 Q15. What is your data retention and secure deletion policy? A: Customer data is retained for the duration of the active subscription plus 30 days, after which it is securely deleted within 60 days using NIST 800-88 compliant methods. Page 16 of 51
4. Network Security Question 16 of 50 Q16. Do you maintain a documented network security architecture? A: Yes. Network architecture documentation is maintained and reviewed as part of the annual risk assessment and ISO 27001 surveillance audit. Page 17 of 51
4. Network Security Question 17 of 50 Q17. Are firewalls and intrusion detection/prevention systems deployed? A: Yes. Arclight deploys next-generation firewalls and a cloud-native IDS/IPS across all production environments. Page 18 of 51
4. Network Security Question 18 of 50 Q18. Is network traffic segmented between production, staging, and development environments? A: Yes. Production, staging, and development environments are fully segregated at the network and account level. Page 19 of 51
4. Network Security Question 19 of 50 Q19. Do you perform regular vulnerability scanning of your network and systems? A: Yes. Automated vulnerability scans are performed weekly, with critical findings remediated within 15 days per our internal SLA. Page 20 of 51
4. Network Security Question 20 of 50 Q20. Is DDoS protection in place for externally facing services? A: Yes. All externally facing services are protected by a cloud-based DDoS mitigation service. Page 21 of 51
5. Application Security Question 21 of 50 Q21. Do you follow a secure software development lifecycle (SDLC)? A: Yes. Arclight follows a documented secure SDLC incorporating security requirements, design review, static and dynamic code analysis, and pre-release security testing. Page 22 of 51
