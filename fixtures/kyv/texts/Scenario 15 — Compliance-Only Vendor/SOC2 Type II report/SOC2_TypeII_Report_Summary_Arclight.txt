Halloran & Pierce LLP INDEPENDENT SERVICE AUDITOR'S REPORT Certified Public Accountants Report Date: April 8, 2025 SOC 2 Type II Report Report on Arclight Cloud Solutions, Inc.'s Description of Its Data Platform System and on the Suitability of the Design and Operating Effectiveness of Controls Review Period: January 1, 2024 - December 31, 2024 Trust Services Categories: Security, Availability, Confidentiality SECTION I - INDEPENDENT SERVICE AUDITOR'S OPINION (Excerpt) In our opinion, in all material respects, based on the criteria described in Arclight Cloud Solutions, Inc.'s assertion: (a) the description fairly presents the Data Platform System that was designed and implemented throughout the period January 1, 2024 to December 31, 2024; (b) the controls related to the control objectives stated in the description were suitably designed to provide reasonable assurance that the control objectives would be achieved if the controls operated effectively; and (c) the controls tested operated effectively to provide reasonable assurance that the related control objectives were achieved throughout the period. No exceptions were noted that were determined to be significant to the overall control objectives during the review period.
