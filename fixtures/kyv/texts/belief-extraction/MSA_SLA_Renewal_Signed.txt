MASTER SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This Master Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 1 September 2025 (the "Effective Date") by and between Northwind Cloud Services, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Vendor"), and Cascade Health Partners LLC ("Customer").

1. Services. Vendor shall provide Customer with access to the Northwind platform (the "Services") as described in the applicable Order Form.

2. Service Level. Vendor guarantees a Service availability commitment of 99.9% uptime, measured monthly, exclusive of scheduled maintenance windows. If Vendor fails to meet this commitment in any calendar month, Customer shall be entitled to a service credit as described in Exhibit B.

3. Fees and Payment. The annual subscription fee for the Services is $180,000, invoiced annually in advance. All invoices are due and payable within thirty (30) days of the invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

4. Term and Renewal. The initial term of this Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues through 31 August 2026. This Agreement shall renew on September 1, 2026 for a successive one-year term unless either party provides Vendor at least sixty (60) days' prior written notice of non-renewal.

5. Confidentiality. Each party agrees to hold the other's Confidential Information in strict confidence and not to disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

6. Limitation of Liability. Neither party shall be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages arising out of this Agreement.

7. Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.
